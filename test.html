FREELANCING

Freelancing entails working for yourself on an hourly or project basis. Freelancers are generally called self-employed since they have the option to choose which projects and firms they want to work with. You're working for yourself and not for a company, but you take work from clients. Selling services are paid per hour; revenues are tied to efforts. Someone who works for different companies at different times rather than being employed by one company.

MEDIA JOBS

Media jobs are specialists who develop and implement all targeted content for various media platforms. A media job specialist is someone who works to show and tell a story, experience, and convey an idea. They're the ones who advertise and promote their team's idea or product.

ONLINE SELLING

Online selling is also known as electronic commerce or internet commerce, which refers to the buying and selling of goods or services using the internet, and the transfer of money and data to execute these transactions. In simple terms, it is selling old clothes, repurposed phones, jewelry, self-designed t-shirts, etc. for example. The seller over the internet allows the consumers to buy goods or products directly from them using a web browser or a mobile app.
